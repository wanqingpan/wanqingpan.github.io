Work

I am a sophomore studying Neuroscience at USC. As an aspiring effective altruist, I am looking for ways to maximize my impact on the world’s most pressing problems through my academicia and career.

At this point in my undergraduate career, my focus has been to gain career capital and transferable skills while furthering my studies into computational neural networks. In the next few years, I am eager to take any opportunity where I can gain fresh perspectives and learn new skills. I am mostly interested in Brain-Computer Inferface, STEM Cell Systems, social entrepreneurship, and emerging markets.

In the long run, I am seeking to combine my experience of living and studying in both China and the US, my interest in tech and entrepreneurship, and my passion for doing the most good into a career that is both a personal niche and an effective service to the present and future people.

Hit me up if you are interested in chatting about effective altruism, China-related topics, emerging techs, global health and development, or just want to get some anime recommendations.
